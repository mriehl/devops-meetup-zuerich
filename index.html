Immune system

  • Antifragility as an emergent effect
  • Harm's only good up to a certain point
  • Reacts strongly to small harm (memory)

Forest fires or how to defeat antifragility

  • Would like to prevent all forest fires
  • Flammable material accumulates
  • Black swan => much worse
  • Small fires would auto-regulate (Feuer√∂kologie)

Key insights

  • The antifragile can slowly adapt to black swan events
  • Relies on some form of tinkering/probability (e.g. mutation, random fire)
  • Pieces from the system must be fragile
  • Manual intervention can defeat fragility

Feedback models (ethics)

  • No skin in the game

    • Your bad decisions lead to other people's harm

  • Skin in the game

    • Your bad decisions lead to your own harm

  • Soul in the game

    • Your bad decisions for the sake of others lead to your own harm

Feedback in IT - password leaks

  • Dev writes password database
  • Customer puts password in DB
  • DB is insecure
  • Customer is harmed
